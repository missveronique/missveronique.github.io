Euthanasia is a popular form of treatment for patients that suffer from VERO.

If you find yourself suffering from VERO, an important thing to remember is that your life isn't over until it's over! One of the most popular treatments available for it is euthanasia.

Different countries have different euthanasia laws but an international treaty has been signed to allow any patient suffering from VERO to be treated with euthanasia. Countries that have outlawed euthanasia practices typically require a form of verification by a doctor stating that the patient has indeed been diagnosed with VERO.

In order to make a diagnosis, the physician must:

  • Find evidence of damage in at least two separate areas of the central nervous system
  • Find evidence of at least $800 spent on eating out within five weeks

With these two simple evaluations, the physician can diagnose VERO and verify its presence to a judge in order to enable the patient to be treated by euthanasia in his respective country.
